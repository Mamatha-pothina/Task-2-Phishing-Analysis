Task 2: Phishing Email Analysis

Objective:
To analyze a phishing email and identify suspicious elements.

Sample Email:
From: support@paypa1.com
Subject: Urgent: Your Account Has Been Suspended!

Dear Customer,
We noticed suspicious activity in your account. Please verify immediately:
http://paypal-security-check.com/login

Phishing Indicators Found:

1. Spoofed Email Address:
The sender email "support@paypa1.com" is fake.

2. Urgent Language:
Uses words like "urgent" and "immediately" to create panic.

3. Suspicious Link:
The link is not an official PayPal domain.

4. Generic Greeting:
Uses "Dear Customer" instead of user's name.

5. Threat Message:
Mentions account suspension to pressure user.

Conclusion:
This email is a phishing attempt using social engineering.
